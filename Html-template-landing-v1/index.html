Frequently Asked Questions

What is Wazo?

▾
Wazo is a secure digital wallet that allows you to manage, send, and receive digital assets easily.

How do I add funds to Wazo?

▾
You can add funds by linking your bank account, credit card, or using local deposit partners.

How can I access my wallet address?

▾
Go to your wallet dashboard and click on “Receive” to see your unique wallet address.

Can I withdraw my funds to my local bank account?

▾
Yes, Wazo supports direct withdrawals to linked local bank accounts.

Can I move funds from wallets?

▾
Yes, you can transfer funds between different wallets or addresses.

Can I import an existing wallet?

▾
Absolutely. Just use your recovery phrase or private key to import your wallet.
